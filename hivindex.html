The purpose of this blog is to educate, inform, inspire, and transform lives of those infected by HIV. This includes positive and negative people. The name of this blog is the POS-Lifestyle. POS being an acronym for Proving Ourselves Special. I chose this title because in reality we are all special, especially those infected with this disease.

A diagnosis of HIV can be life-shattering and life-changing all at one time. Those of us who have it know the struggle to succeed and maintain a healthy lifestyle everyday even when we are exhausted and our body just hurts from the medications we take. Yes, many would say this is a curse, but HIV is by no means the death sentence it used to mean. At times it feels like a double edge sword, we have this life altering diagnosis yet at the same time we have been given an opportunity to improve our lives and health in a way that most people will probably never do. In doing so we help fight the stigma on HIV and bring education and awareness throughout the world.

There is no reason any one infected with HIV shouldn’t live the long happy life negative people do if we take care of ourselves. This blog is also meant for everyone. With time I will add translations in multiple languages for the convenience of the world. Yes, it focuses on a target group, However the content within these pages can help improve anyone’s life. Cause in the end aren’t we all trying to Prove Ourselves Special?

The Contents of this Blog

This site is designed to improve every one’s life one day at a time. Throughout, you will find focused workouts with a specific goal in mind. These goals include but are not limited to; gaining more energy, losing weight, building immunity, help sleep and more.

You will also find nutritional information and recipes to promote a healthier lifestyle, along with countless News Resources on related topics of this blog.

There is also information on spiritual improvement, financial stability and more. I am working on an app which will include features such as medication reminder alarms, portable work out routines, News, etcetera. I will keep you all posted on this app.
